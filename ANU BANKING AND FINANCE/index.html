    About Us

    Anu Banking and Finance is one of the leading public sector banks of the country. The Bank is a listed entity and the Government of India holds 90 percent in Bank’s total share capital. The Bank, having its headquarters at Mumbai (India), was registered on November 9, 2001 as a limited company. Today, it has a network of 9500+ domestic branches, 13300+ ATMs, 11700+ BC Points, serving over 120 million customers with 75000+ employees having 3 branches overseas at Hong Kong, Dubai International Financial Centre (UAE) & Sydney; 1 representative office in Abu Dhabi; 1 banking subsidiary at London , 1 banking joint venture in Malaysia; 3 para-banking subsidiaries and 3 joint ventures.The Bank has received several awards and recognition for its prowess in technology, digital banking, financial inclusion, MSME and development of human resources.
